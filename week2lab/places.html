My Favorite Places

Tawau, Sabah

My hometown. Foods are cheap and tasty and diverse. Roads are less confusing.

Kota Kinabalu City

Bustling city in Sabah with many things to do and many places to explore. One of the main tourist spots in Sabah.

Tanjung Aru Beach, Kota Kinabalu

One of the many tourist spots in Kota Kinabalu. Sunsets here are beautiful and amazing. Popular "healing" place.

Kuala Lumpur City

Many interesting places and cafes to go to that's not available in Sabah. A lot of my friends are mostly living/studying nearby so it's the most convenient place to hang out at.

Desa Dairy Farm, Ranau

Playfully known as "Little New Zealand" of Sabah. Home of the local "Desa" milk brand. The farm is popular with both locals and tourists. Visitors can buy fresh dairy products and participate in many activities.

Melaka City

A very interesting and historical city. Went here as a kid and visited several historical figures. Also went on a boat ride along Sungai Melaka with my family. Must visit again in the future.
